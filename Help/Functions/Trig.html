Trigonometric Functions

BASeParser supports a wide variety of functions- and, of course, these include functions that deal with trigonometry. Alongside the base level Sin(Sine), Cos(Cosine), and Tan(Tangent) functions, BASeParser supports all manner of Hyperbolic and Inverse functions. The following table summarizes them:

Function NameDescription
SinSine. Takes an angle and returns the ratio of two sides of a right triangle. The ratio is the length of the side opposite the angle divided by the length of the hypotenuse.

The result lies in the range -1 to 1.
